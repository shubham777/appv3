About Us

RentBingo -- The one stop solution to renting a myriad of products. In the city for only a few months? Or wife likes to keep changing furniture? Or your kid is contesting a fancy dress competition? Don't worry! We take away your hassle of buying stuff that you need only once (here you can have 'buying stuff that you won't use again'). Our business is mutual. You rent us your worries and we rent out the goods. Furniture to Fancy dresses; Appliances to Adventure gears -- You name it, we rent it. Browse ahead and drop us a mail or a call, We are a whisper away from our cell phones. We look forward to serving you soon ;).
